About this home

Set on a quiet, tree-lined street in Leaside, this 2.5-storey detached offers about 2,300 square feet across four finished levels. The main floor carries soaring 11-foot ceilings and oversized windows, keeping the living, dining, and family rooms full of light. It backs onto a school at the rear. No rear neighbours.

The eat-in kitchen anchors the main floor: built-in Miele appliances, a large centre island with seating, and an open-concept flow into the family room that overlooks the backyard. Hardwood runs throughout the ground level.

Upstairs, three bedrooms include a primary with a cathedral ceiling, a four-piece ensuite, and a walk-out to one of two private terraces. A third-floor loft with a skylight works as an office, a gym, or a fifth bedroom.

The finished lower level adds flexibility: a separate entrance, a full second kitchen, a recreation room with a gas fireplace, and another bedroom, plus a walk-up to the landscaped, fully fenced backyard. It's a strong fit for an in-law suite or rental income. Recent upgrades, per the sellers, include 200-amp service, a new Carrier furnace and A/C under warranty, renovated second-floor bathrooms (2025), and turf with irrigation front and back. Move-in ready.
